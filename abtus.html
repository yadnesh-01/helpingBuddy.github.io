About HelpingBuddy



HelpingBuddy is a Non Goverment Website launched in 2023 by PAY2S Web Developers. This website helps people to find the NGO's, orphan ages, old age homes, animal shelter's in the respected area they search for. The website also works for making the donations to such institutes not only in India but worldwide. This website made the making donations easier where donor also gets the report of the use of their donation so they can ensure of the fair use by institutes.
